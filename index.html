Reading 2 – City Guide
Exam style A / B / C

Read the guide to three places in the city. For questions 1–6, choose the correct place: A, B or C.

A River View Wheel
B Sefton Park
C City Sports Stadium
A · River View Wheel
Near the port and the main square

This enormous wheel is sixty metres tall. When it turns, you can see the river, the airport and the lights of the shopping centre. The ride lasts about fifteen minutes. There is a small café under the wheel with wifi, so you can go online while you wait.

B · Sefton Park
A green area for quiet walks and bikes

This park is perfect when you are tired of the noisy main square. There are trees, fountains and a big lake where you can rent a boat. There is a café with wifi and a small information office. The park is open twenty-four hours a day and it’s free to visit.

C · City Sports Stadium
For football matches and big concerts

This sports stadium is near the bank and the post office. People come to see football matches at the weekend. On concert days, there are thousands of people. There is a restaurant inside, but you can’t bring your own food or drinks. After a match, you can walk to the main square in ten minutes.
