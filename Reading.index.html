Interactive Reading Practice – Exam Style

Exercise 1 – T / F / DS

Read the text. Then choose T (true), F (false) or DS (doesn’t say).

Smart Umbrella

Today, many people use special apps to check the weather, but a young inventor called Mia wanted something different. She designed a “smart umbrella” for a school project. The umbrella has a small light on the handle. When the weather is sunny, the light is green. When it is cloudy, the light is yellow. When the light is red, it is going to rain.

Mia worked on her invention after school and at weekends. Her best friend helped her to test the umbrella on rainy days. They walked around their town and wrote notes in a small notebook about the weather and the colour of the light.

Mia’s science teacher was very impressed and sent a photo of the umbrella to a local newspaper. A journalist wrote an article about Mia, and people in her town started to ask where they could buy the umbrella. Mia doesn’t sell the umbrella now, but she wants to improve it in the future and maybe make a company one day.

  1. Mia invented a special app to check the weather.
  2. The smart umbrella uses different colours for different weather.
  3. Mia only worked on the umbrella during her science lessons.
  4. Mia and her friend tested the umbrella outside in their town.
  5. A local newspaper wrote about Mia’s invention.
  6. Mia already has a big company that sells umbrellas.
